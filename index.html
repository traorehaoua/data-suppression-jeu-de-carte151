Account and Data Deletion – Card Game 151

Card Game 151 is an online card game application that allows users to play, progress, and interact. Authentication can be done via Facebook, Google, or email and password.

Collected Data

We collect and store the information necessary for the game to function:

  • Profile information (name, email address, profile picture if provided by the authentication provider).

This data is used solely to provide you with a personalized gaming experience and to improve our services.

Deleting Your Account and Data

You have the right to request the deletion of your account and all associated personal data. To do so, please contact us directly using one of the methods below. We will process your request as soon as possible (typically within 7 business days).

Please include the following in your message:

  • Your username or the email address used for the account.
  • The authentication method used (Facebook, Google, or email/password).
